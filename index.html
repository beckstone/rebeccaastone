A little bit about me.

I've had a robust career designing books in-house for imprints of HarperCollins, Macmillan, Random House and Reader’s Digest and designing books and magazines as Associate Art Director for Topix Media Lab. I have worked with the biggest and best creative partners, including Disney, Nickelodeon, Mother Goose Club, and many others. When I am not designing books, designing websites, or illustrating, a good portion of my time is devoted to my 13 year old daughter who has developed a passion for makeup. I also play violin in community orchestras.
